Nicodranas is the first and one of the major port cities on the [[Menagerie Coast]]. The city is under the control of the [[Clovis Concord]].

The five Nicodranian councillors are: 
- Lord de Montfort
- Lady Sherome
- [[Gellan Primewater]]
- Misses Donegal
- [[Commodore de Modesto]]
